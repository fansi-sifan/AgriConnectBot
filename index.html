About AgriConnect

AgriConnect is a pioneering no-code platform designed to empower farmers by democratizing access to vital agricultural knowledge. Through platforms like WhatsApp and Telegram, it provides real-time, tailored advice on climate-smart practices, pest management, and crop diversification, all in the user's native language.

Features

  • Real-time agricultural advice
  • Accessible through WhatsApp and Telegram
  • Content in native languages
  • Interactive chatbot scripts and radio content
  • Enhances productivity and sustainability

Impact and Vision

AgriConnect aims to improve livelihoods, bolster food security, and build resilience against environmental challenges. Its impact extends to entire rural communities, promising improved yields, sustainable practices, and an empowered agricultural workforce.
